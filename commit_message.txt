COMPREHENSIVE REFUND FIX - ALL AFFECTED AREAS

1. Donor's total donated amount
2. Campaign total raised and progress
3. Charity total donations received
4. All platform-wide statistics
5. Leaderboards and rankings
6. Dashboard analytics
7. Badge calculations
